Behind the Lens

The Story

I am a visual artist and photographer based in New York, with over a decade spent chasing light across six continents. My work explores the quiet tension between the human form and its environment \u2014 the decisive moment where emotion becomes geometry.

Whether shooting the windswept dunes of Namibia or the rain-soaked streets of Tokyo at 3 AM, I believe every frame is a negotiation between what is seen and what is felt. I shoot mostly on film, with occasional forays into digital when the moment demands it.
